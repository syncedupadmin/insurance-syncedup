Frequently Asked Questions

How does the Global Leaderboard increase sales? +
Competition drives performance. When agents see their national ranking against other agencies, they naturally push harder to climb higher. Our data shows a consistent 47% average sales increase across all agencies using the Global Leaderboard system.
Is there a setup fee or contract? +
No setup fees, ever. No contracts required. We include white-glove onboarding, data migration, and training at no additional cost. You only pay the monthly subscription and can cancel anytime with 30 days notice.
Can I upgrade or downgrade my plan? +
Yes, you can change your plan at any time. Upgrades take effect immediately, and downgrades take effect at your next billing cycle. We'll prorate any differences automatically.
What integrations are available? +
We integrate with all major CRM systems, lead sources (Boberdoo, QuoteWizard), and carrier systems. Professional and Enterprise plans include custom integrations and API access for your specific needs.
How quickly can we get started? +
Most agencies are fully operational within 2-7 business days. Our team handles all the technical setup, data migration, and training while you focus on running your business. Enterprise setups may take 2-4 weeks for custom features.
What kind of support do you provide? +
All plans include email support. Professional and Enterprise plans get priority phone/video support, dedicated account management, and custom training sessions. We also provide comprehensive documentation and video tutorials.
